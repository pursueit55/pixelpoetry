Meet the Designer

The Heart Behind the Studio

Michelle Evans is the designer and owner of Pixel Poetry Studio.

She fell in love with web design the moment she realized she could take someone’s vision and turn it into something beautiful they could share with the world. For her, there’s a kind of magic in blending art and code until a website feels just right.

Having poured countless hours into mastering her craft, Michelle treats every project as if it matters — because to her, it does. When a client trusts her with their website, they’re trusting her with how the world sees their business, and that’s a responsibility she takes to heart.

She built Pixel Poetry Studio on a simple belief: everyone deserves a website as unique as they are. No generic templates, no cutting corners — just beautiful, custom work made with genuine care.
